# Data Source Monitoring

Navigating to Tenant Inventory:

1. In the **CybrHawk Analyst Console**, click the **hamburger menu** in the top-left corner.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (36).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Select **My SOC** from the menu.

Under **SOC Interactions and Monitoring**, click **Data Source Monitoring**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-01-21 at 4.16.14 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You should now see this page.
