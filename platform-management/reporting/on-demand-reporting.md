# On Demand Reporting

To generate reports manually:

* Log in to the customer portal
* Choose the report you wish to export
* Select reporting period, or a custom date:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-05-13 141116.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Generate an initial summary with AI. Click **Edit** if you require changes, click **Save**, and then click **Export** to generate a PDF.
